    Trevor Bauer loses tough game

    The Dodgers co-ace lost another tough game last night, giving up two solo home runs to the Houston Astros. The Dodgers offense once again could not muster a lot of runs, something that frequently happens when Bauer takes the mound.

    Bauer gave up solo homeruns to Jose Altuve and George Bregman and exited the game having thrown 100 pitches. The fiery right hander ended with a respectable statline of 6 IP, 4 H, 2 R, and 3 strikeouts. The Dodgers' winning streak ended at 8.





    Los Angeles Clippers in deep trouble

    The Clippers did themselves no favors after last week's debacle which saw them lose games deliberately to OKC and Houston. It was all part of a grand master plan to avoid the cross-town Lakers. Instead the Clippers got a first round date with a familiar foe, the Dallas Mavericks. And after watching the first two games, it seems that the strategy has backfired. Big time.
